# Audience packs

Curated entry points into The Haven's policy library, grouped by who's reading.

- **[For Commissioners and Schools](commissioners-and-schools/)** — the policies and procurement documents Local Authority and school-based commissioners need
- **[For Families](families/)** — the parent- and carer-facing policies and guides
- **[For Haven Staff and Volunteers](staff-and-volunteers/)** — induction reading and annual sign-off set
- **[For the Designated Safeguarding Lead](dsl/)** — the safeguarding-relevant set with statutory references

Every page in an audience pack links to the same canonical policies under [`policies/`](../policies/) — these packs are curated views, not duplicates.
